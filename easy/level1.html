Lemon Juice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4-6 lemons
  • 1 cup of water
  • 2-3 tablespoons of sugar (adjust to taste)
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Squeeze the lemons to extract the juice.
  2. Strain the juice to remove seeds and pulp.
  3. Add water and sugar to the lemon juice. Stir well.
  4. Pour over ice and enjoy!
